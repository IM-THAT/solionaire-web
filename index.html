What is Solionaire

Your wallet.
Your empire.

Solionaire is a Solana Mobile app that turns your SOL holdings into a real estate empire. The more SOL you hold, the higher your tier — from street-level hustler to penthouse elite.

Claim territories on-chain with a Memo transaction. Build your daily streak to unlock Midas Touch. Rise through 10 levels across New York and Dubai.

How it works

Three steps to your empire

🔗
Connect Your Wallet
Link your Solana wallet via Mobile Wallet Adapter or Seed Vault. Your SOL balance determines your tier automatically.

Build Your Empire
Swipe daily to collect Lustre points and maintain your streak. Hit 7 days to unlock Midas Touch — the golden multiplier.
